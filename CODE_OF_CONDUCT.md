# Code of Conduct

Eye of Horus follows the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct, version 2.1.

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected Behavior

- be respectful and precise;
- critique ideas, not people;
- distinguish speculation from evidence;
- help newcomers understand the research status of the project.

## Enforcement

Reports may be sent to contact@agentflow-enterprise.com. Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when behavior harms the community.
